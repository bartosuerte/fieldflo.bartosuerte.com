# Interview Prep

Everything for the FieldFlō Senior Product Designer loop, in one place, built to read on a phone. The notes pull from the company research, the role, the interviewer dossiers, and the design-fundamentals primer. The throughline across every page is one honest claim: an AI-native designer who ships. Fifteen years turning messy into clean, now building end to end with Claude, with working software to prove it.

> [!NOTE]
> One north star, said plainly: "I am not a product designer learning AI. I am an AI-native builder who does the work of product design, end to end, and has done that substance for fifteen years under different titles." Lead with proof, own the one gap when asked, never inflate.

## The case study, in one line

Spend most of the time on interaction and UX, a little on FieldFlō-aesthetic polish, and keep a live decision log so you can answer the three questions every reviewer asks: your process, your read of the problem, and why you chose the solution you did. Lean on Claude for breadth, then say out loud where your judgment overrode it.
