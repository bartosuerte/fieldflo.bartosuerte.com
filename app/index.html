How it works

↳ Honest by design - because reasoning clearly about AI is the job

Parsing: Your files are read entirely in your browser (pdf.js for PDFs, mammoth.js for Word, native for text). Nothing is uploaded - no server, no storage, no tracking.

Grading: A client-side relevance engine scores the documents against six categories derived from the FieldFlō posting, citing the exact phrases that matched. It's a real signal-coverage model - not a black box, and not rigged. A weak application will score weak, mine included.

Optional live Claude: the same UI can call real Claude through a small server-side proxy for generative grading and answers - off by default to keep this free. This whole tool was itself designed and built with Claude.
