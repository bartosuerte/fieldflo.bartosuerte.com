## STAR story: the Humble and Fume rebuild

- **Situation:** Joined as Creative Marketing Director. The e-commerce and marketing infrastructure was outdated legacy systems, and wholesale customers could not really order online given complex, flexible terms.
- **Task:** Modernize it so wholesale ordering worked online, and improve efficiency.
- **Action:** Designed and built the integrated stack (Webflow, Klaviyo, Zapier wired into NetSuite), mapping quote to PO to inventory to SO to fulfillment. Led a small team of junior creatives and partnered with the Director of Operations for accuracy. Framed it as a deliberate low-code architecture choice, not a duct-tape job: off-the-shelf e-commerce could not handle wholesale terms, and a full custom build was slow and expensive.
- **Result:** A streamlined online experience, a 20%+ lift in online sales, and efficiency and satisfaction gains. The money line: "It is the same instinct behind FieldFlō. The founders did not pay six figures for custom SaaS, they built what they needed. I did a smaller version of that."
