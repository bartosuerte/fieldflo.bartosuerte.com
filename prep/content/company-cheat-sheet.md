# Company Cheat Sheet

A one-page printable summary of FieldFlō: what they do, who they serve, the funding, the module suite, and the headline numbers. Use it as a fast refresher right before a round. The full detail lives in the [FieldFlō Profile](#/company-profile) and the [Competitive Battlecard](#/competitive-battlecard).

[FieldFlō Cheat Sheet (PDF)](assets/FieldFlo-Cheat-Sheet.pdf)

## The 30-second version

- **What:** AI-native vertical SaaS for specialty subcontractors. One platform, around 13 modules. Built by contractors, for contractors.
- **Who:** the DA&R trades (Demolition, Asbestos Abatement, Environmental Remediation), plus Masonry and Concrete in the marketed verticals.
- **Backing:** Mainsail Partners, roughly $35M, October 2025. Legal entity The Maker System, LLC. Founded 2016, HQ Denver, 11 to 50 employees.
- **North star:** accessibility that transcends age, language, education, and technical expertise.
- **Wedge:** more vertical than Procore, broader than point tools. Specialized and consolidated.
- **Headline ROI claim:** about $79K average annual savings (marketing figure, treat it as their claim).
- **The live design problem:** extend FlōTime's near-zero-friction sign-in standard across every module, for older, multilingual, lower-digital-literacy crews.

> [!TIP]
> Framing rule: the company is FieldFlō. FlōTime is just one of its modules (time tracking and payroll). Never call the company FlōTime.
